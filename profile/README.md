# AltrioPay

There's a tradition in Naples that most people outside Italy have never heard of.

It's called **caffè sospeso** — "suspended coffee." When you order your espresso, you pay for two. One is yours. The other is left behind, anonymous, for someone in need who walks in later and asks for "un caffè sospeso." No questions asked. No paperwork. Just quiet solidarity.

In a world where payment systems are broken for the unbanked, where crypto demands technical knowledge and where gas fees punish the poor — this tradition felt like the answer to a question no one was asking the right way.

---

## We built AltrioPay

A payment gateway that runs on Solana, settles in USDC, and needs zero SOL to transact. Your gas? Paid in stablecoin — like cash, but digital.

But that's not what makes us different.

What makes us different is the **sospeso** — the ability to pay it forward. Not just for coffee. For any product. Any amount. One payment, split: half goes to you, half stays behind for someone else. The recipient claims it privately. No身份, no KYC, no bank account required. Just a wallet.

You can buy one coffee and leave one suspended. You can buy ten meals and leave five for strangers. You can fund a school lunch for a kid you've never met — and they'll never know who you are.

We're bringing the oldest act of anonymous generosity in Europe into the oldest Money Legos in crypto.

---

## Why Solana?

Three reasons:

1. **USDC settlemet** — Every transaction is stable. No volatility. Your $1 is always worth $1.

2. **Zero-gas in stablecoin** — Traditional crypto makes the unbanked pay gas in a token they don't have. AltrioPay pays gas in USDC from the transaction itself. The sender doesn't need SOL. The receive doesn't need SOL. Nobody touches volatile assets unless they want to.

3. **Private by default** — Solana's speed means we can ring-fence transactions. No public ledger browsing for predators, no address exposure, no financial surveillance dressed as transparency.

---

## The vision

Caffè sospeso works because it's simple, anonymous, and optional. No bureaucracy. No applications. Just human to human.

AltrioPay is that same idea — but for the entire economy. Products. Services. Needs. Wants. A paid-forward economy built on Money Legos, where nobody gets left behind because they couldn't afford the fee.

**Pay for yourself. Pay for someone else. No one has to know.**
